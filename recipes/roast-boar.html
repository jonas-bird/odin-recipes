Roast Boar Leg

How to roast a Boar Leg

Some might say that the classic roast boar leg is a stereotypical and simplistic dish. I on the other hand say it is a classic!

Civilized Preparation

If you want to cook a boar the way a soft city dweller who has never chopped the head off of a troll or defended his long-ship from a sea serpent than I recomend Broken Arrow Ranch's recipe they look sensible and tasty

The Proper way

Sauce? they.. put... sauce on a boar?!? Well the only sauce we need is the tears and lementations of our fallen foes!

Ingredients

  • A roaring fire
  • One fresh wild boar
  • two large horns of mead
  • tears and lamentations of fallen foes

Steps

  1. Go to forest (make sure to bring a spear or the boar may be eating you)
  2. Find one large boar (not too large...)
  3. ...survive
  4. have someone dress the boar and any major wounds
  5. light large fire
  6. roast boar
  7. drink mead
  8. eat boar
  9. drink mead
